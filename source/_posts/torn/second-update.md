## 🤼 Join a faction and find new friends
Last year on October 14, two days after I published my first Torn post, I received a message from **Will**. It says that they're recruiting bunch of people for their factions. It's up to us if we join or not. If I join, I have to choose from a list of factions and I chose ***Orange Chocolate Winter***. You may ask why.. It is because there are only two people in there that time and I want to start with only a few people in a group. Right now, we have 11 people on the faction (including me). They are so welcoming, understanding and helpful people. And being on a faction is really a blessing because we have a faction bank where we could deposit money and stuff, and withdraw or loan from it. We also help each other during fights, wars, ect.

## 🏡 Upgraded living
Remember on my prevoius blog, I've said that I was writing it on my apartment? Now, I'm writing this blog post on my mansion. That's right!

On my previous work, I've got $50k salary daily. I kept 90% of it on my faction's bank. Aside from that, I also have done different side jobs for George and an online bussiness. I kept doing that until I can finally bought a mansion. It had now a market price of $5M+ and an upkeep of $500 daily. Inside of it, there are hot tub, sauna, large pool and open bar. I also spent $1.5M for superior interior modification.

I live here alone. I didn't hire staff yet so I do the cooking, the cleaning... all the household chores you can think of! Well, that's part of being an independent individual, ya know!? I have to get through this for now.

My dream is to live on a private island someday with my partner (if there's gonna be one but if there's none, it's fine) and by that time, maybe I can afford hiring staff.

## 💼 Same job, different company
Today, on Torn, is [Employee Appreciation Day](https://www.torn.com/newspaper.php#!/articles/2573)! Shoutout to all the employees out there like me.

I'm still working as a security contractor but on a different firm. I AWOL on my previous firm because of personal reasons and I had to leave Torn. My previous director, fired me but she leave me a message that said that if ever I want to come back to work, I can just let her know. When I came back to Torn, I couldn't contact her so I applied to a bunch of firms. I work to army as a Major while waiting for a company to hire me. It took days before I got accepted to a firm with a daily salary of $500k. That's so much bigger than on my previous firm.
